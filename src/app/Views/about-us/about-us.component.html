ABOUT US

Chronowiz.com launched in late 1999, initially offering for sale only new and unworn brand name watches at discounted prices. More recently we have expanded by accepting trades and offering preowned watches as well. Our goal is to provide our customers with a pleasant shopping experience, while maintaining great prices and exemplary customer service. Over the years we have earned recognition as an honest and dependable watch dealer. We are known as the most trusted online source for discounted brand name watches. By all means, please research our company; we are confident you will come to the same conclusion as thousands of other satisfied customers. We look forward to welcoming you to the Chrono Wiz family of customers.

We offer watches for sale via 2 separate divisions within our company:

www.chronowiz.com - New/Unworn Watches:
We have a substantial inventory of in-stock items ready to ship the same or next business day. However, while not all watches advertised are in stock, most are typically available to be ordered with a relatively short delivery time, ranging from 2-3 days to a few weeks. Some watches must be special ordered or might be on back order, which will delay delivery time, in which case we will notify you with an estimated delivery time within 1-2 business days of receiving your order. If your order is time sensitive, we encourage you to contact us before placing an order, to confirm availability.

www.chronowiz.com - Preowned and Vintage Watches:

All watches advertised are in stock and available to ship out the same or next business day. The pictures of the preowned watches are always live pictures of the actual watch we are offering. We make an effort to display multiple high resolution images of the actual watch in order to accurately portray the true condition. We further make an effort to accurately describe the overall condition of the watch so that customers can get a feel of what the overall condition is in real life. All preowned watches will come with either a 1-year ChronoWiz.com warranty for modern watches or a 6-month ChronoWiz.com warranty for vintage watches. Every watches that we offer has gone through an accuracy test, a water resistance test and general cleaning of the case. At times we might have the watch refinished, in which case we will make sure to disclose that in the description. We accept trades and we actively buy preowned and vintage watches.

chronowiz.com does NOT sell replicas, fakes or watches that have had their serial numbers removed or altered. chronowiz.com guarantees that all the watches we sell are 100% genuine with the original serial number intact.
